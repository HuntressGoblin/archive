    <h3><i class="fa-chisel fa-regular fa-book-open"></i>What is the Cluster Galaxy?</h3>
    <p>
        The Eternal Library is a vast, ever-expanding container of <a class="internal-link" href="The Tomes of the Eternal Library">tomes</a> that form living, breathing universes through the use of writing formed from <a class="internal-link" href="The Ink that Binds">Ink</a>. These <a class="internal-link" href="The Tomes of the Eternal Library">tomes</a> are stored within archives that act as <a class="internal-link" href="The Cluster Galaxy">galaxies</a> throughout the inky void. The <a class="internal-link" href="Istoria, Lady of the Ink">Storyteller</a> resides in this domain as each word is scribbled down to breathe life into the world it guides. No mortal should be able to arrive to this realm, but it isn't impossible if someone's story is given a chance to not be cut short... ...but only once, and it's quite rare. <br> <br>

    <h3><i class="fa-chisel fa-regular fa-book-open"></i>Why don't the Cluster Galaxies Overlap?</h3>
    <p>
        Despite the <a class="internal-link" href="The Tomes of the Eternal Library">tomes</a> being held within the Eternal Library, the universes are kept separate. They cannot interact with each other due to one phenomenon called <a class="internal-link" href="The Ink that Binds">Ink</a>. The Ink creates a barrier that prevents the worlds from blending together. It is a rarity if anyone makes it across, and all who do never come back to tell the tale to their original <a class="internal-link" href="The Cluster Galaxy">galaxy</a>.
    </p>
