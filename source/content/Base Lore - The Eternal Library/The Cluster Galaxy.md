### Why the Name "Cluster" Galaxy?
The name of the Cluster Galaxy has two main reasons. For those who are mortal and live within its realm, the "Cluster Galaxy" refers to how the planets and planes come together to form the universe around them, just like how a cluster of planets can form a solar system. However, the real reasoning goes beyond what any mortal or even divine mind can comprehend. The "Cluster Galaxy" is named due to the "cluster" of tomes that form the world. These clusters form the various aspects of a universe such as planets, characters, plant life, history, and other small pieces that contribute to the world.
