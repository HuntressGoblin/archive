> Ink makes up everything in [[The Cluster Galaxy|the Cluster Galaxy]]. To be more accurate, ink is what makes up the writing that forms [[The Tomes of the Eternal Library|the tomes of the Eternal Library]]. This includes anything pertaining to these forms of writing, whether it be the universe, other characters, objects, etc. All beings are made of writing, which is what the ink uses to form the universe.

---
Ink makes up everything in [[The Cluster Galaxy|The Cluster Galaxy]] through the use of words. When something is written, ink makes it come to life. If one were to get their hands on even a small amount of ink, they could create anything their heart desired. Fortunately, such a feat is statistically impossible due to ink being imperceivable, similar to atoms except that even looking at the smallest parts of one's being would reveal no signs of being made from the inky words of a story. 

Ink acts like a divider between each part of [[The Cluster Galaxy|the Cluster Galaxy]]. It prevents the various universes from interacting, which is important considering how wildly the various systems, creatures, and different forces of each world can vary.

Ink cannot be destroyed, nor can it be created. **It just is.** The only way it can be manipulated to reveal other universes is due to [[The Supernova|the Supernova]]. A signal of the end times itself, but what is it for [[Istoria, Lady of Ink| the Lady of Ink]] to care? Stories go on, no matter how horrible the universe becomes. For every source of evil, there are heroes to counter it.
