The Eternal Library is a great expansive of shelves full of many tomes. Some tomes lie untouched, some unfinished, others fully done, but most are still being written. Within the endless expanse of both nothingness and ink, feather quills float to continue noting down every little detail in each story the Eternal Library holds. When books are no longer being written in, or perhaps the tale is done, the book will be placed in an archive. These archives are used to sort out each book and keep them preserved and easy to find.

---
The archives of the Eternal Library have a magical property. The content within the tomes stored within forms "clusters" that create universes. Each detail is utilized to create a living, breathing world. The "clusters" that are formed create what is referred to as [[The Cluster Galaxy|the Cluster Galaxy]].


---

Every story is safely stored in the Eternal Library, watched over by a large figure cloaked in purple. Some refer to this figure as the "Domain Master," but others may refer to them as "[[Istoria, Lady of Ink]]." To see Istoria is to see madness itself, be wary not to look behind the paragraphs.
