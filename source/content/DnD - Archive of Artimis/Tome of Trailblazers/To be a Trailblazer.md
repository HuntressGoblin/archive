<div class="goldbox"> I think there’s a lot of interesting ways to think about trailblazing. There is no doubt the indomitable spirit of humanoid creatures desires as much power as they can snatch between their little fingers… or lack of fingers… fins, paws, yada yada yada, I ain’t keeping track anymore! There’s a lot of hardship and victory that can come from leaving a trail for others to follow, leaving some kind of legacy no matter how small. When you think of the work “trailblazer”, what do you think of? Personally, I see a path burning and blazing bright, a trail of fire to light the way and guide further action from within the blinding darkness of life. Now, let us open this tome with a cover that shimmers. Welcome everyone… to the story of Trailblazer. Let’s start with the first chapter, shall we?

  </div>
<p> Greetings and Salutations, </p>
Esteemed person-of-interest, I write to you with this enchanted letter to bring you a special message. Read close, for this letter is long in length and content. I, Yin Pahlavan, am funding an operation into an unexplored continent many, many miles far upon the settled lands we all know. This land, dubbed Eternity, poses a mysterious challenge. These lands have long been famed for their mysterious nature and the lack of survivors who return from their voyages. The rumors of this island present it in a most promising light, with tales of power, wealth, and knowledge lost in the archives of time itself. The truth of the situation, however, is quite unknown. You, along with seven other adventurers, have been selected due to a few key sources proving that you possess skills that can foster something great: a kingdom. My proposition to you is simple: voyage to these unknown lands and become a trailblazer, forging a new kingdom that will last for the eons to come. Within this letter, you should find a silver ticket marked with the Silverquill logo. Present this ticket to Captain Swift-at-Sea within the city of Waterdeep. They’re a short-haired tabaxi with lightly colored fur. The workers around the docks can give you directions if you cannot find him. They will allow you onboard and shall escort you there, alongside plenty of supplies to aid you in your journey that will cost you not a single coin. Upon you receiving this letter, you will have six ten-days to prepare for the journey. If you are interested, follow the instructions written on the ink of this letter. The vessel will leave at the end of the assigned time limit above. If not, disregard it and lose the opportunity to find what you have been seeking for so long. I wish you well.
