  <div class="letterbox-header">
    The Letter that Started it All
  </div>
<p> Greetings, </p>
You may not know me, but I am very aware of you. I am aware of your situation and want to help. Someone like you has a lot of potential, potential that can blossom into something legendary. I am Yang Pahlavan, and I have something very valuable that may interest you for a favor. A friend of mine, Runara, resides at Stormwreck Isle and is facing an increasing amount of disruption and danger. She resides in Dragon’s Rest, a place intended to be a respite from the world’s horror for all who live there. There has been an upsurge in unusual activity on the island: from undead to odd magical happenings. She believes that there is a force of evil causing this, and this villainy cannot go unpunished.
<p> If you are interested, you have two tendays to get to the ferryman who will bring you to the Isle. He resides off the High Road near the location marked upon this letter. Find Runara and assist her, use whatever you deem to be necessary to complete this task and restore justice. Once this is complete, I shall meet you to grant you both your reward and perhaps a future partnership.
  </p> <div class="letterbox-signature">
    — Best Wishes, Yang Pahlavan.
  </div>

> [!dragon]- How did it begin?
> Six heroes once roamed the lands. Each was burdened with a horrible problem that threatened to swallow them whole. All seemed lost, until a day when a letter appeared before them. A man named Yang addressed them personally, promising to help if they assisted him with his own issue. The party agreed, beginning their adventure to fight against the ultimate form of tyranny.

> [!dragon]- What is the year and location of this campaign? 
> This campaign took place on Stormwreck Isle in the Spring in 1493 DR. Over the span of the campaign, the party resides mostly on the Sword Coast during the same timeframe.
