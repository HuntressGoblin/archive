    <div><strong><i class="fa-regular fa-book-open-reader"></i> Summary</strong></div>
    <div>Belle was born in <a class="internal-link" href="Horseshoe Ranch">Horseshoe Ranch.</a> She resides there for a majority for her life, but she eventually left for new adventures to head to <a class="internal-link" href="Little Lake">Little Lake</a>. She keeps her face covered up constantly. Appearance-wise, she has brown and white fur with a saddlebag and a large hat. She has a large shawl that she uses to hide her face and two gun holsters strapped to her hip that hold her pistol and shotgun.</div>
    <br> Belle is the daughter of Mayor Wilde. She’s 21 years old. She’s got less fur than her father and takes more in appearance from her mother, but she’s got her father’s temper and sense of adventure. She wants to get out of town and see the world. Particularly, she is a valuable asset in helping commoners build their own farms and ranches. She is able to keep food afloat somewhat through her own skills.
    <br> <br> Personality-wise, Belle may seem a bit intimidating by her appearance. Yet, she is actually a bit of an excitable ditz who wants to see the world.
