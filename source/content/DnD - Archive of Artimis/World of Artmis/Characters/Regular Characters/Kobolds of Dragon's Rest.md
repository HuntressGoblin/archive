<div class="npc-section">
<div class="npc-section-title">Personality</div>

<div class="markdown-rendered">

- **Agga** speaks little and has no patience for nonsense. She keeps the rest of the kobolds organized and in line.
- **Blepp** has a sharp danger sense and is convinced he’s supernaturally lucky. His prized possession, his lucky dagger, was sold to [[Kalis]] for 100 gold pieces.
- **Frub** has limitless energy and desperately needs help finding productive directions to channel it. He loves to ask questions about everything other people are doing.
- **Kilnip** has terrible insomnia and sleeps only a few hours each day. She is always tired but an eager conversationalist.
- **Laylee** has a curious mind and a talent for tools and building. She serves as Myla’s helper.
- **Myla** is a winged kobold tinkerer. Her wings were injured by sturges, which she refers to as "hungry, icky, blood-sucking, bat-things." Runara helped her recover. Myla now spends her time experimenting with inventions, alchemy, and magic.
- **Rix** is pious and tends to the temple, acting as Runara’s assistant. She adores puns.
- **Zark** is rude and fond of colorful insults. His favorites are “Eat my sword, bugbear breath!” and “Your father was a gas spore!” He is indifferent toward visitors.

</div>
</div>

<div class="npc-section">
<div class="npc-section-title">Bonds</div>

<div class="markdown-rendered">

- [[Elder Runara]] - Runara is a leader figure to the kobolds that they all follow religiously. Something about her appeals to them, and she treats them well. 
- [[Rise of Tyranny|Yang Gang]] - The kobolds perceive this group of adventurers positively, especially Blepp, who has a large collection of gold thanks to them.
- [[Sparkrender]] - The kobolds fear this blue dragon wyrmling due to his cruelty and evil.

<div class="npc-section">
<div class="npc-section-title">Summary</div>

<div class="markdown-rendered">

Various kobolds reside on [[Stormwreck Isle]] within [[Dragon's Rest]]. They each have their own personalities, hobbies, and differences. Each of the kobolds honors peace and compassion, just as [[Elder Runara]] had taught them.

</div>

<div class="markdown-rendered">

Each of the kobolds has their own backstory, although many of them were apart of the roaming kobold tribes on [[Stormwreck Isle]] until [[Elder Runara]] took them in.
