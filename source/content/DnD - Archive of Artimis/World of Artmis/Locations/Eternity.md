> # Eternity - Where the Adventurous go to Vanish

> <div class="continent-info"><strong>Name</strong> Eternity</div>
> <div class="continent-info"><strong>Age</strong>Formed in 1150 DR.</div>
> <div class="continent-info"><strong>Climate</strong> Always Shifting. One part of the land can be freezing cold, while nearby it can be incredibly hot. At the last day of every month for 24 hours, a <a class="internal-link" href="Skyfall">skyfall</a> occurs.</div>
> <div class="continent-info"><strong>Summary</strong> Eternity is a massive continent hidden out in the vast oceans on <a class="internal-link" href="Artimis">Artimis</a>. Nobody has ever went to the island and returned to tell the tale. Many believe that there is untold treasure, power, and wealth. Some believe it is cursed and that anyone who travels there shall die. The only fact that anyone knows about the island is that anyone who has gone there has vanished for what feels like... eternity. The most noticeable thing about this land is the <a class="internal-link" href="Stardust">stardust</a> that seems to emanate from it.</div>
